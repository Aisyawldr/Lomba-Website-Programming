Business Intelligence

BI or Business Intelligence is a profession tasked with processing and analyze business-related data so that it can help the company makes the right decisions.

Learn More

What is Business Intelligence ?

Business Intelligence (BI) is also known as Business Intelligence which refers to the systems, applications and technologies used to collect data from various sources, processed and displayed in form of information which is usually in the form of an easy-to-read summary, comprehensive and accurate method used to measure and analyze also to monitor operations within the agency or company with the aim of helping decision makers (management) in order to make more effective decisions

Why Need to Build Business Intelligence?

To identify the company's level of achievement

BI supports companies to achieve their strategic vision by aligning all levels to focus on activities that are directly related to the strategic vision.

To dig deeper into existing data sources

Many companies fail to explore and utilize their data fully, while BI can provide all strategic operational data and performance metrics to improve the quality of decision making which can also improve the quality of company performance.

To monitor company performance and identify problems

BI provides insight into company performance at various levels. BI provides information about performance issues within the company.

The future of BI as the company's business driver

BI is a very important element in today's business world and in the future, without BI, it will be difficult for companies to identify problems that occur and it is difficult to measure the company's performance if If not addressed, it will continue to harm the company and will be more difficult win market share even though the business strategy is good.
